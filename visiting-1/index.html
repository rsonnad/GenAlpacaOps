Event Parking Instructions

Welcome to the Austin Alpaca Playhouse! We're excited to have you.

Important: Parking

Please read these parking instructions carefully to help us maintain good relationships with our neighbors.

Parking Options

Option 1: Gravel Lot (Limited Spaces)

When you come in the gate, you can park in the gravel lot on the right if there is space available.

  • Park facing either the street or the container
  • Park near other cars – don't be a space hog
  • This fills up quickly during events

Option 2: Street Parking (Preferred for Events)

If the gravel lot is full:

  1. Exit the property and turn left
  2. Go past two houses
  3. Park on the street facing the highway
  4. DO NOT park in front of any houses

Option 3: Frontage Road (Overflow)

If street parking is full:

  1. Turn left before the highway onto the frontage road
  2. Park to the right of the signs that say "no parking to the left"
  3. This is about a 5-minute walk to the property

DO NOT

  • DO NOT park in front of neighbors' houses
  • DO NOT park on the grass – there are sprinklers and you'll get stuck if it has rained ($200 towing bill)
  • DO NOT park on the circle or by the garage – this blocks Amazon deliveries
  • DO NOT park in front of the sauna or garage

Address & Directions

160 Still Forest Drive, Cedar Creek, TX 78612

We are 13 minutes east of the Austin Airport, a block off of Highway 71.

When You Arrive

  1. Drive through the gate (it may be open for the event)
  2. Park according to the instructions above
  3. Walk in and enjoy!

Quick Rules Reminder

  • No alcohol (unless it's your wedding)
  • No meat inside the house
  • Keep gates closed – the alpacas will escape!
  • Clean up after yourself
  • Don't call the alpacas "llamas" – they take it personally

For full visiting rules, see the Visiting page.
