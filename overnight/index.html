Overnighters Guide

Guidelines for overnight residents at the Playhouse

Welcome to the Austin Alpaca Playhouse community

Principles

Throughout history, successful co-living communities have been built on the principle that the interests of the individual are enhanced by the condition of the commons. Each of us at the Austin Alpaca Playhouse is a steward of the commons. We each contribute to enhance our shared environment through mindful attention to shared resources, invitations, and community gatherings.

As a member of the Alpaca community we hope you resonate with the following principles:

  • Respect the Commons – Treat shared spaces as your own, maintaining cleanliness and order
  • Contribute – Give back to the community through your presence and participation
  • Embody – Live the values we share
  • Gratitude – Appreciate the opportunity to be part of this community
  • Wisdom – Make thoughtful decisions that benefit everyone

Logistics

Kitchen

  • Refrigerator: You may use the bottom section of the kitchen refrigerator, the doghouse refrigerator, or the freezer in the garage.
  • No meat inside: You can put meat in the doghouse refrigerator (not the garage), and you can cook meat outside.
  • No alcohol (unless it's your wedding)
  • Label your food with your name and date
  • Clean as you go: Wash dishes immediately after use, don't leave them in the sink

HVAC & Climate

  • The main house has central AC/heating controlled by thermostats
  • Cabins and outbuildings have individual climate control
  • Please be mindful of energy usage
  • Close doors and windows when AC/heat is running

Laundry

  • Washer and dryer are available in the garage
  • Please remove your clothes promptly
  • Clean the lint trap after each dryer use

Trash & Recycling

  • Take trash out when full – don't let it overflow
  • Recycling bins are next to the trash
  • Compost available for food scraps

Visitors

Please do not invite guests without asking a resident. All guests must read the Visiting page.

  • Give the "alpacaplayhouse.com/visiting" link any time you want to invite someone over
  • Never give the ADDRESS – never post the address. NEVER please – this always causes problems
  • Do not invite others to stay overnight unless there is an email confirmed agreement in place with a resident
  • Do not let guests sleep in common areas

Shopping & Supplies

Nearby shopping options:

  • H-E-B – 15 minutes in Bastrop
  • Walmart – 15 minutes in Bastrop
  • Amazon deliveries – Please don't block the circle or garage

Basic supplies (toilet paper, paper towels, dish soap) are shared. If you use the last of something, please replace it or let a resident know.

Departure

When you leave:

  • Strip your bed and leave linens in the laundry area
  • Clean your space – leave it better than you found it
  • Remove all personal items – check drawers, bathroom, fridge
  • Take out any trash from your room
  • Return any borrowed items to their proper place
  • Close and lock doors and windows

Looking for a Room?

Furnished rooms are available for rent at the Alpaca Playhouse for short or long term occupancy.
